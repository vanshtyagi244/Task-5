Biography

Film Actor

Sanjeev Tyagi is a Indian Television Actor, born on 29 June 1971 in Uttar Pradesh, India. He is mainly known for his role of Senior Police Inspector in Crime Patrol Series at Sony TV and Policeman in Baby(2015). Sanjeev Tyagi live in Mumbai. He started his career with DD national TV shows. Later Sanjeev Tyagi done movies like Money Devo Bhava, Baby(2015) and Hanak(2021) etc. And Shows like Karam Dharam Apna Apna(2009), Kahaani Ghar Ghar Kii(2000), Raavan(2006), Junoon - Aisi Nafrat Toh Kaisa Ishq(2012), Court Room: Sachchai Hazir Ho(2019) And Crime Patrol. He married to Dipali in 2001. He playing role of Abhimanyu Jindal in Crime Patrol.
